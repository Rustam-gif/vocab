# Vocabulary Builder

**Build your vocabulary, one word at a time.**

---

## What is this app?

Vocabulary Builder is a simple, focused app for learning new words. Whether you're studying a foreign language or expanding your English vocabulary, this app helps you learn and remember words through short daily practice sessions.

No complicated grammar lessons. No flashy games. Just straightforward vocabulary practice that fits into your day.

---

## Who is it for?

- Adults learning a new language
- Busy people who want to learn in small daily chunks
- Anyone who wants a simple, no-fuss way to build vocabulary

---

## How it works

1. **Learn new words** — Words are introduced in small, manageable batches
2. **Practice daily** — Quick exercises help you remember what you've learned
3. **Track your progress** — See your streak, scores, and improvement over time

That's it. Simple and effective.

---

## Key features

**Start without signing up**
Try the app as a guest. Create an account later if you want — your progress stays with you.

**Daily practice sessions**
Short sessions designed for busy schedules. A few minutes a day is all you need.

**Smart review system**
The app tracks which words you know well and which need more practice, so you review efficiently.

**Progress that syncs**
Sign in to save your progress across devices. Or stay offline — it works either way.

---

## What this app is NOT

- Not a full language course
- Not a grammar teacher
- Not designed for children

This is a focused tool for one thing: building your vocabulary.

---

## Get started

Open the app and start learning. No account required.

When you're ready, sign up to save your progress and access it anywhere.
